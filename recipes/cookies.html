Best Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe Ever

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg + 1 egg yolk (room temp)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar 100 grams
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar 150 grams
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/4 cup all purpose flour 280 grams
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/2 tsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cup semisweet choocolate chips 225 grams

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325° F.
  2. Whisk together flour, baking soda, cornstarch, and salt together in a large bowl. Set aside.
  3. Whisk melted butter with sugars until there are no lumps. Whisk is the egg and then the egg yolk. Finally whisk in the vanilla.
  4. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Cover and store in fridge overnight (2-3 hours minimum)
  6. Make 30 gram balls and bake at 325°F for 12 minutes.
  7. Let cool on wire rack for 8-10 minutes.
