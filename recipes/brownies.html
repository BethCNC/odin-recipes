Chewy Gooey Brownies

Ingredients

  • 5 tbsp unsalted butter 71 grams
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar 249 grams
  • 2 large eggs plus 1 egg yolk, cold
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil 74 grams
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder 75 grams
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour 63 grams
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup semisweet choocolate chips 128 grams

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325° F. Line an 8x8 inch light colored metal pan with foil or parchment paper and spray with nonstick cooking spray
  2. In a microwave safe bowl, add the butter and sugar. Microwave for about 1 minute, or until the butter is melted.
  3. Whisk in the eggs and egg yolk, whisking vigourously for 1 minute.
  4. Whisk in the vanilla.
  5. Then stir in the oil and cocoa powder
  6. With a rubber spatula, stir in flour, cornstarch, and salt until combined.
  7. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  8. Spread the brownie batter evenly into the prepared pan. Place in the oven and bake for 30 minutes, or until brownies are set.
  9. Do not overcook. Let cool completely to solidify before cutting and serving.
  10. Store in airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days.
